Biography

Dr. Shanshan Feng is currently a Senior Scientist II at the Centre for Frontier AI Research, Institute of High Performance Computing, A*STAR, Singapore. He is working with Prof. Yew Soon Ong. He received the Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University (Data management and Analytics Lab, Supervisors: Prof. Gao Cong and Prof. Yeow Meng Chee), Singapore in 2017, and obtained his B.E. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2011. His current research interests include generative AI, big data analytics, graph learning, and recommender systems. He has published more than 50 papers in prestigious journals and conferences including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TKDE, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TGRS, SIGKDD, SIGIR, ICDE, VLDB, etc. He served as a PC member of SIGIR, SIGKDD, ICDE, ACM MM, WWW, AAAI, IJCAI, ICDM, CIKM, SDM, and DASFAA etc.
